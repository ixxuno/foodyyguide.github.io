Food & Drink Guides

Find the best food and drinks for studying and relieving stress.

Best Food for Studying

Nuts and Seeds

Benefits: Rich in protein and healthy fats, perfect for sustained energy and brain function during study sessions.

Blueberries

Benefits: Packed with antioxidants that improve memory and cognitive function, ideal for focus.

Dark Chocolate

Benefits: Contains flavonoids that boost brain plasticity and improve learning ability. Great for motivation!

Green Tea

Benefits: A natural stimulant with a gentler effect than coffee, it enhances alertness without the jitters.

Best Food for Stress Relief

Avocados

Benefits: Rich in healthy fats that help reduce stress by lowering cortisol levels in the body.

Oats

Benefits: High in fiber and can help reduce blood sugar levels, preventing anxiety spikes. They also have a calming effect.

Salmon

Benefits: Rich in omega-3 fatty acids, it helps regulate stress hormones and promotes overall brain health.

Chamomile Tea

Benefits: Known for its calming properties, chamomile tea can help reduce anxiety and induce better sleep.
