Main Dishes

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

A simple yet flavorful pasta dish with garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes. Perfect for a quick and delicious meal.

Ingredients:
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 tsp chili flakes
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
Instructions:
  1. Boil a pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the garlic slices and cook until golden brown and fragrant (about 2 minutes).
  3. Add the chili flakes and cook for another 30 seconds.
  4. Once the pasta is done, reserve 1/4 cup of pasta water and drain the spaghetti.
  5. Add the drained spaghetti to the pan with the garlic and oil mixture. Toss everything together, adding pasta water if needed to help coat the spaghetti.
  6. Season with salt and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve immediately!
Chicken Alfredo

A creamy pasta dish with tender chicken and rich Alfredo sauce. Comfort food at its best!

Ingredients:
  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 250g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
Instructions:
  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Cook in a skillet over medium heat until browned and cooked through (about 6-7 minutes per side). Remove from heat and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  3. Add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat and stir in parmesan cheese until the sauce thickens.
  4. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package directions, then drain, reserving a little pasta water.
  5. Slice the cooked chicken and add it to the sauce along with the cooked pasta. Toss everything together, adding pasta water as needed to adjust the sauce's consistency.
  6. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately!

Veggie Spring Rolls

Fresh, crunchy, and full of healthy vegetables. These spring rolls are perfect for a light snack.

Ingredients:
  • Spring roll wrappers
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup rice noodles (optional)
  • Soy sauce for dipping
Instructions:
  1. Soak the spring roll wrappers in warm water for 10â€“15 seconds until soft.
  2. Place the soaked wrapper on a flat surface and layer it with shredded cabbage, carrots, cucumber, and cilantro.
  3. If desired, add rice noodles for extra texture.
  4. Roll up the wrapper tightly, folding in the sides as you go.
  5. Serve with soy sauce for dipping!

Drinks

Lemonade

A refreshing and tangy drink perfect for hot summer days.

Ingredients:
  • 4 lemons, juiced
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 liter water
  • Ice cubes
Instructions:
  1. In a small saucepan, combine 1 cup of water with sugar. Heat over medium heat until the sugar dissolves, creating simple syrup.
  2. In a pitcher, combine lemon juice, simple syrup, and the remaining water.
  3. Stir well, then chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Serve over ice cubes and enjoy!
